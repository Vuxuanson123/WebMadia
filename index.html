About us A clever creative agency
with an appetite for hospitality
and lifestyle.

We think outside of the box to create bespoke campaigns that engage and excite. We launched in 2007 with the goal of helping tell our clients’ stories in a compelling way.

With an ever changing media environment, these days success for us means creating conversation both in the news and in the world of social. To do this, we’ve put together a talented team with a background in PR,design, photography, social, marketing, and journalism.

We use whatever channel we can to get your message out there – and our long-established connections help us do this. Whether designing a brands logo, hosting a launch event, or pitching to the media, we inject our signature Wasamedia energy and excitement into everything we do. Our roots are in hospitality - we love what we do and go above and beyond to achieve success for our clients. We’re dedicated to delivering on what we promise.
